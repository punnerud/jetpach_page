Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 10, 2025

Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how JetPach ("the App") handles information. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience using our app.

Information Collection and Use

JetPach does not collect, store, or share any personal data or information from its users. All your packing lists, tags, and settings are stored locally on your device only. We do not require you to create an account or provide any personal information to use the app.

Data Storage

All data you create in JetPach — including packing lists, items, tags, and notification settings — is stored exclusively on your device. No data is transmitted to external servers. Your lists are private and remain under your control at all times.

Third-Party Services

The app uses anonymous usage analytics to help us understand how the app is being used and to improve the experience. This data is completely anonymous and cannot be linked to individual users. No personal information is collected or shared through these analytics services.

Notifications

JetPach can send local notifications to remind you about packing. These notifications are scheduled locally on your device and are not processed by any external service. You can disable notifications at any time in your device settings.

Children's Privacy

Our app does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under 13 years of age.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
