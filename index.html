JetPach

Pack smart, jet far

Never forget what to bring. JetPach helps you organize packing lists for every occasion — from gym sessions to game day. Tag, filter, and get reminded on time.

Lists for Every Occasion

Build custom packing lists for any activity. Add items quickly and check them off as you pack. Clear checkmarks when done and reuse the same list next time.

Organize with Tags

Assign color-coded tags like "Football", "Training", or days of the week. Filter your lists by tag to quickly find what you need.

Inherit from Other Lists

Going to a match? Include your training list automatically so you never miss the basics. Nest lists inside each other to avoid duplicating items.

Smart Reminders

Set up weekly recurring notifications or one-time reminders. Get notified before your activity so you have time to pack.

Built for Simplicity

Fast and straightforward. No account required. All data is stored locally on your device. Your lists are private and always available, even offline.

Drag and Drop

Reorder items however you like with intuitive drag and drop. Keep your most important items at the top of the list.

Features

  • Create unlimited packing lists
  • Check off items as you pack
  • Organize lists with color-coded tags
  • Filter lists by tags and days of the week
  • Include items from one list in another
  • Weekly recurring and one-time notifications
  • Change history log for each list
  • Reorder items with drag and drop
  • English and Norwegian language support
  • No account or sign-up required
  • All data stored locally on your device
